About CloseNet

CloseNet was created to help real estate agents save time, look professional, and generate more leads without struggling over design.
I’m an independent designer with a strong interest in real estate marketing and digital products.
I noticed that many agents lose hours trying to create visuals, manage content, or keep their branding consistent, especially now when social media is a huge part of our lives, while their real job is selling and closing deals.
CloseNet exists to remove that friction.

What I Focus On

Instead of complex tools or expensive custom work I focus on practical ready-to-use design assets built for real estate professionals.

• Present listings clearly and professionally
• Stay consistent across social media and marketing
• Save hours every week on design decisions

Everything is built with real estate workflows in mind simple efficient and results-focused.

Why CloseNet

CloseNet is not an agency and not a large company.
It’s a focused project built to support real estate professionals who want better visuals without wasting time or energy.

If design slows you down CloseNet is here to help you move faster.
