Open House Flyer Design

A modern, high-impact open house flyer designed to attract attention both online and in print. Created specifically for real estate agents who want to promote listings professionally and effectively.

What It’s Designed For:

Social media promotion (Instagram, Facebook, stories)
High-quality printing for open houses, offices, and local distribution

Features:

Clean, eye-catching layout that highlights the property
Clear information hierarchy for date, time, and location
Versatile design suitable for both digital and print use
Easily customizable with property details and agent information

Why It Works:

Helps create buzz and drive traffic to open houses
Looks professional across all platforms
Saves time while delivering a strong visual impact
Reinforces trust and credibility with potential buyers

How it works

Download the PDF file – inside, you’ll find a link to the EPS file.
Download the EPS file – this is your editable flyer template.
Open in Adobe Illustrator – customize text, colors, images, and layout however you like.
Print or share digitally – your flyer is ready to go!
No design skills needed — just download, edit, and promote your open house in minutes!
